LAKE GARDA WEDDING

Travel

The easiest way to travel to Lake Garda is via Air, there are five possible airports to use however they vary in distance. The preferred airport to use is Verona, this airport is located approx. 1 hour from Malcesine by car. Venice is the next nearest but requires a 2 hour drive to Malcesine. It is possible to travel to Malcesine by bus from the airports and some information is included in the link below but we would recommend a hire car as the easiest method, they are available at reasonable rates from the airport.

• Verona Catullo (VRN) • Milan Bergamo (BGY) • Milan Linate (LIN) • Venice (VCE) • Milan Malpensa (MXP)
